GOV.UK Coronavirus

Designing the custom homepage during the Coronavirus pandemic

  • Designed multiple iterations of the Coronavirus homepage in response to changing user and government needs, as the pandemic progressed.
  • Redesigned important core features of the page, including statistics, and functionality that allowed users in different nations within the UK to toggle guidance for them.

Below: Some considerations that went into the design of the Coronavirus nation picker. The nation picker allowed users in England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland to show only rules and guidance relevant to them.

In the end, we decided to display all the nations on the top level rather than hide options under a button or link, to avoid someone missing the link and seeing information meant for another nation.
